Our History

Founded in 2005, Global Care Foundation began with a mission to combat hunger in underprivileged communities. Over the years, our organization has expanded its reach to include education, healthcare, and sustainable development projects in over 30 countries worldwide.

Our Mission

To empower communities worldwide by providing access to basic needs, fostering education, and enabling sustainable growth.

Our Vision

A world where every person has the opportunity to live with dignity and purpose.
